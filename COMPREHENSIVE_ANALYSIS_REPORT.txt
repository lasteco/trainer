================================================================================
HAND NOTATION STANDARDS
================================================================================

Valid Formats:
1. Pairs: Two identical ranks
   Examples: AA, KK, QQ, JJ, TT, 99, 88, 77, 66, 55, 44, 33, 22

2. Suited Hands: Higher rank + Lower rank + 's' (lowercase)
   Examples: AKs, AQs, AJs, KQs, KJs, QJs, JTs, T9s, 98s, 87s, 76s, 65s, 54s

3. Offsuit Hands: Higher rank + Lower rank + 'o' (lowercase)
   Examples: AKo, AQo, AJo, KQo, KJo, QJo, JTo, T9o, 98o, 87o, 76o, 65o, 54o

Critical Rule: All suffix letters MUST be lowercase!
❌ INVALID: A3O, Q9S, K8S (uppercase)
✓ VALID:   A3o, Q9s, K8s (lowercase)
